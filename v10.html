information about Plant Island

The island is located in the heart of the Nile River opposite the city of Aswan, and extends as an open garden containing hundreds of species of rare plants and trees that were brought from different parts of the world more than a cen10 The island features wide walking paths winding through mini forests, palm groves, and shaded resting areas, making it a favorite destination for tourists and locals seeking tranquility in nature. Its plant collection includes ancient trees, medicinal and aromatic plants, rare bamboo types, cacti, and various imported tropical species. The island also offers spectacular views of the Nile and the mountains of Aswan, providing visitors with a visually captivating experience. Through the years, the Aswan Botanical Island has evolved into an environmental and cultural symbol, serving as a platform for environmental awareness and the exchange of knowledge about tropical and subtropical plants. It is considered one of the most important botanical gardens in Africa and the Middle East, and one of Aswan’s most iconic landmarks, embodying the beauty of nature and the richness of Egypt’s environment.
